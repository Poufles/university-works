Discover, Explore, and Immerse into the Known Unknowns of the World
France
Elegance, finesse, fashion, art, gastronomy, and many more; name it all! France is the land of diverse culture conceived by its incredible regions and people. A country that one would wish to be travel to.
Barcelona
Beaches, seaside hotels, and theatrical entertainments; including colorful, lively music can all be found in Barcelona, Spain. A place of history, culture, and commerce to open the mind to more perspectives.
Boracay
Known for its white sand, it is a great place to go in vacation and relax with its maginificent seaside view accompanied by rows of palm trees sided by bars and restaurants to socialize; and a specific place to do recreational activities.
Chocolate Hills
A range of 1,268 hills of the same size created through a unique geomorphic transformation recognized as the Philippine's 3rd National Geographic Monument in 1988. It also include recreation activities such as zip lines, trailing, comping, and team building.
Rice Terraces
A complex mand-made geoform sculpted to create a magnificent layered mountain composed of planted rice. It is a place of rich rice culture coated by a vast mountain range perfect for sightseeing and the sensation of nature.
Baguio City
A way to chill and feel the cold in the equatorial archipelago of the Philippines. It is the place of strawberries, floral parks, pine forests, rich culture, and mountainious view.
