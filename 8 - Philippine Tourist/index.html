Exquisite Cuisine

Experience the vibrant flavors of Cordillera cuisine! Discover a rich tapestry of local ingredients, traditional cooking techniques, and unforgettable dishes that showcase the unique heritage of the region. From savory smoked meats to hearty vegetable stews, each bite tells a story of cultural exchange and culinary innovation. Join us on a gastronomic journey through the mountains of Cordillera and savor the tastes of tradition and authenticity.

PINIKPIKAN


Traditional Filipino dish, Pinikpikan, features smoked chicken, pounded before cooking, resulting in tender, flavorful meat with unique smoky taste.

PINUNEG


Traditional Filipino blood sausage, made with pork blood, meat, and spices, cooked to perfection on the grill or in a pan.

KINIING AT ETAG


Cordillera's prized smoked meats, crafted with ancestral methods, bursting with authentic mountain flavors.

KINIWAR


Filipino sweet treat crafted from sticky rice, coconut milk, and sugar, offering a delectable caramelized flavor in every bite
