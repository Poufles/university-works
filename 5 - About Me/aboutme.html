What Can I Do?

Layout

The process of organizing elements and similar ideas into sections promoting a specific product with emphasis. It creates a boundary between two different concepts to aid clients what characteristics and features the product has.

Structural Design

The form taken by a webpage to understand the heirarchy flow of information in order to show priorities leading the client to understand the most important and the summary of a certain product.

Organization

The format taken by elements in a section helping clients to distinguish where to read and rest encouraging attraction and curiousity. It divides elements into chunks and fragments for the clients to consume easily.
